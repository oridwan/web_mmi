ElectrideFlow - High-Throughput Inorganic Electride Discovery Workflow

Accelerated by Generative Models and Hierarchical Prescreening: A four-stage intelligent workflow for high-throughput inorganic electride discovery.

Stage (a)

Chemical Exploration

Generative AI models (MatterGen) explore electron-rich composition space with excess valence electrons (0 < Nexcess ≤ 4) from electropositive alkaline, alkaline-earth, and early transition metals.

Stage (b)

Stability Screening

MatterSim (MLP) pre-screening filters structures by energy above hull to identify thermodynamically promising candidates (Eref-hull-MLP < 0.10 eV/atom).

Stage (c)

Electride Identification

Coarse DFT relaxation with ELF and PARCHG topological analysis to identify electride candidates with localized interstitial electrons (Bader volume > 20 A^3).

Stage (d)

Refinement & Validation

Tight DFT relaxation with refined convex hull analysis for final candidate validation, identifying thermodynamically stable electrides (Ehull-DFT < 0.05 eV/atom).

About LEGO-xtal

LEGO-xtal (Local Environment Geometry-Oriented Crystal Generator) is a symmetry-informed AI generative approach for crystal structure prediction. Our method generates diverse crystal structures using machine learning and optimizes them using structure descriptors rather than traditional energy minimization.

1,700+ Structures

Expanded from 25 known sp2 carbon allotropes to over 1,700 low-energy candidates

AI-Powered

Symmetry-informed generative models trained on augmented datasets

ML Optimization

Optimized within 0.5 eV/atom of graphite ground state

Generalizable

Adaptable to MOFs, battery materials, and modular building blocks
